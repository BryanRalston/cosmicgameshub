How to Play RIFT

RIFT has three zones. Complete all three to finish today's puzzle.

⚡ CHAIN

Change one letter at a time to transform the start word into the end word. Fewer steps = more stars.

🌱 GROW

Add one letter at a time to grow a word from 3 to 6 letters. Each step must be a valid word.

🔮 DECODE

A word is hidden behind clues. Reveal as few clues as possible before guessing — fewer clues = more stars.
