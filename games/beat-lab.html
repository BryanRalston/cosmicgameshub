How to Play

BEAT LAB is a rhythm tap game. Hit notes as they land on the target line.

  • Notes scroll down the lanes — tap when they hit the glow line
  • On desktop: press D F J K for each lane
  • On mobile: tap the lane buttons at the bottom
  • Perfect = 100 pts, Good = 50 pts, OK = 10 pts
  • Build combos for bonus multipliers
  • Unlock Pro Mode by completing songs
