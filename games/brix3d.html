🎮 Getting Started

Build amazing LEGO creations in your browser! This tutorial will guide you through the basics.

  • Orbit Camera: Click and drag to rotate the view
  • Zoom: Scroll wheel to zoom in/out
  • Pan: Right-click and drag to pan
💡 The sidebar on the left contains all your building tools and brick options!

🧱 Placing Bricks

Select a brick type from the sidebar, then click on the build area to place it.

  • Select Brick: Click any brick button in the sidebar
  • Place: Click on the ground or on top of existing bricks
  • Rotate: Press R to rotate before placing
  • Colors: Pick a color from the palette before placing
💡 Bricks automatically snap to the grid and stack on top of each other!

🛠️ Editing Tools

Use these tools to modify your creation:

  • 🗑️ Delete Mode (X): Click bricks to remove them
  • 🎨 Paint Mode (P): Recolor existing bricks
  • 🖌️ Face Paint (Shift+P): Paint individual brick sides
  • 💧 Color Dropper (I): Pick color from existing brick
  • ↩️ Undo (Ctrl+Z): Undo last action

🏗️ Quick Building Tools

Build faster with these powerful tools:

  • 🧱 Wall Builder: Click and drag to create walls instantly
  • ⬛ Floor Builder: Drag to fill areas with plates or tiles
  • 🪞 Mirror Mode (M): Symmetrically place bricks
  • 👻 Transparent (T): Make bricks see-through
💡 Wall builder lets you set height and auto-creates staggered brick patterns!

👤 Walk Mode

Explore your creation from a first-person perspective!

  • Enter Walk Mode: Press V or click Walk Mode button
  • Move: WASD keys
  • Look: Move your mouse
  • Jump: Space
  • Place Bricks: Click or press Enter
  • Open Menu: Press Tab for brick selection
💡 Use Page Up/Page Down to adjust placement height!

📦 Prefabs & Saving

Use pre-built structures and save your work:

  • Prefabs: Find ready-made structures in the Prefabs section
  • Search: Use the search box to find specific prefabs
  • Save (Ctrl+S): Download your creation as a file
  • Load (Ctrl+O): Open a saved creation
  • Clear All: Start fresh (in Edit menu)

⭐ Tips & Tricks

  • Quick Slots (1-5): Access favorite bricks instantly
  • Weather: Try different weather effects for ambiance
  • Starter Worlds: Begin with pre-built scenes
  • Gravity (G): Drop bricks realistically
  • Selection Box: Click and drag to select multiple bricks
  • Press ?: Show keyboard shortcuts anytime
💡 Have fun building! There's no wrong way to create.
